# Synthetic Court Ruling Allowing Home Office Deduction

ECLI:NL:RBAMS:2024:456

## Facts

Paragraph 7. The taxpayer used a separate room exclusively for remote consulting work and maintained daily usage records.

## Legal Question

Paragraph 8. The court had to decide whether exclusive business use plus detailed records satisfied the statutory deduction conditions.

## Reasoning

Paragraph 12. Exclusive business use and contemporaneous records established a sufficient factual basis for deduction of the business portion of home office costs.

## Holding

Paragraph 16. The deduction was allowed because the taxpayer proved business use and maintained reliable supporting records.
