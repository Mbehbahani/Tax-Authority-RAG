# Synthetic Supreme Court Home Office Ruling

ECLI:NL:HR:2023:123

## Facts

Paragraph 10. The taxpayer claimed home office expenses for a room used for both administrative business work and private storage.

## Legal Question

Paragraph 11. The question was whether mixed private and business use satisfies the statutory requirement for deductible home office expenses.

## Reasoning

Paragraph 14. Mixed private and business use does not automatically defeat deductibility, but the taxpayer must provide reliable records establishing the income-producing portion of the use.

Paragraph 15. Where records are absent, the authority may reject the deduction because the factual basis for allocation is missing.

## Holding

Paragraph 18. The court upheld denial of the deduction because the taxpayer did not provide reliable contemporaneous records.
