# Synthetic Court Ruling on Mixed Home Office Use

ECLI:NL:GHSHE:2022:789

## Facts

Paragraph 5. The taxpayer claimed a deduction for a room used for both home storage and occasional bookkeeping.

## Legal Question

Paragraph 6. The issue was whether incidental business activity in a mixed-use room was sufficient for home office deductibility.

## Reasoning

Paragraph 10. Incidental business activity did not outweigh the room's private function, and the taxpayer did not establish a reliable allocation basis.

## Holding

Paragraph 14. The deduction was denied because the room was not shown to be predominantly used for income-producing activity.
