# Synthetic Supreme Court Records Estimation Ruling

ECLI:NL:HR:2021:321

## Facts

Paragraph 3. The taxpayer presented reconstructed spreadsheets after the audit but no contemporaneous invoices or logs.

## Legal Question

Paragraph 4. The court considered whether reconstructed records alone could justify estimating a deductible home office amount.

## Reasoning

Paragraph 9. Estimation is not allowed where the statutory documentation duty is a precondition for deduction and the taxpayer failed to keep contemporaneous records.

## Holding

Paragraph 13. The authority was entitled to reject the deduction because post-hoc reconstruction did not satisfy the documentation duty.
