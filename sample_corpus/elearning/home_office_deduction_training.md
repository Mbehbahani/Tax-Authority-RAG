# Synthetic Home Office Deduction Training

## Module 1 — Basic Concepts

Paragraph 1. Home office questions should be answered from current legislation, applicable case law, and approved internal guidelines.

Paragraph 2. If the available authorized sources do not contain an exact rule, staff should not infer a fiscal conclusion.

## Module 2 — Citation Practice

Paragraph 1. Each answer must cite the source document, article or section, and paragraph supporting the claim.
