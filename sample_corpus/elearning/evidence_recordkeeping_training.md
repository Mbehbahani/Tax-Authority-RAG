# Synthetic Evidence and Recordkeeping Training

## Module 1 — Source Documents

Paragraph 1. Supporting evidence for a deduction should identify the date, amount, supplier, and business purpose of the claimed expense.

Paragraph 2. Usage logs are especially important where a cost must be allocated between private and business use.

## Module 2 — Reliability

Paragraph 1. Contemporaneous evidence is generally more reliable than reconstructed evidence created after an audit starts.
