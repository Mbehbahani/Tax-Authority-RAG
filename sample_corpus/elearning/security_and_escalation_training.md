# Synthetic Security and Escalation Training

## Module 1 — Access Boundaries

Paragraph 1. Staff must answer questions only from sources they are authorized to access under their role, clearance, and need-to-know scope.

Paragraph 2. If a user request concerns fraud, criminal investigation, or FIOD material, staff must escalate rather than improvise an answer.

## Module 2 — Safe Response

Paragraph 1. When authorized evidence is missing, the correct response is abstention or escalation, not speculation.
