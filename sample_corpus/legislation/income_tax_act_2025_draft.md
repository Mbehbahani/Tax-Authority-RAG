# Synthetic Income Tax Act 2025 Draft Amendment

## Chapter 3 — Income from Work and Home

### Section 3.1 — Draft Deduction Rules

#### Article 3.12 — Home Office Expenses

Paragraph 1. The draft 2025 rule would require exclusive or nearly exclusive business use for a simplified home office deduction.

Paragraph 2. Draft provisions must not be applied to tax years before their effective date unless enacted transitional law states otherwise.

Paragraph 3. Helpdesk staff must not present draft provisions as binding law.
