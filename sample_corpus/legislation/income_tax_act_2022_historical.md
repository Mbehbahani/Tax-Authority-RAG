# Synthetic Income Tax Act 2022 Historical Version

## Chapter 3 — Income from Work and Home

### Section 3.1 — Deductible Work Expenses

#### Article 3.12 — Home Office Expenses

Paragraph 1. For tax year 2022, a taxpayer could deduct home office expenses only when the workspace was exclusively used for business activities.

Paragraph 2. For tax year 2022, the taxpayer had to maintain dated records describing the business use of the workspace.

Paragraph 3. This historical article expired on 2022-12-31 and must not be applied to later tax years unless a transition rule is retrieved and cited.
