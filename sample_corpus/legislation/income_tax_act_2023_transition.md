# Synthetic Income Tax Act 2023 Transitional Version

## Chapter 3 — Income from Work and Home

### Section 3.1 — Transitional Deductible Work Expenses

#### Article 3.12 — Home Office Expenses

Paragraph 1. For tax year 2023, a home office deduction required regular and predominant business use of the workspace.

Paragraph 2. The taxpayer was required to keep dated records showing business use, private use, and the method used to allocate mixed expenses.

Paragraph 3. If the taxpayer could not separate private and business use, the deduction had to be denied unless a transitional approval applied.

#### Article 3.14 — Transitional Approval

Paragraph 1. A transitional approval could apply only when the taxpayer had written confirmation from the authority before filing the 2023 return.
