# Synthetic Tax Records Retention Regulation 2024

## Chapter 2 — Administrative Evidence

### Section 2.2 — Retention and Audit Trail

#### Article 2.20 — Retention Period

Paragraph 1. Taxpayers must retain source records supporting deduction claims for seven years after the filing date.

Paragraph 2. Digital records are acceptable if they preserve date, amount, supplier, business purpose, and integrity of the original record.

#### Article 2.21 — Audit Logs

Paragraph 1. When a deduction depends on use over time, the taxpayer must keep a usage log or equivalent contemporaneous evidence.

Paragraph 2. Reconstructed records created only after an audit request have lower evidentiary weight than contemporaneous records.
