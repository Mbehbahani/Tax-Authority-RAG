# Synthetic Income Tax Act 2024

## Chapter 3 — Income from Work and Home

### Section 3.1 — Deductible Work Expenses

#### Article 3.12 — Home Office Expenses

Paragraph 1. A taxpayer may deduct a home office expense only when the workspace is used primarily for income-producing activities during the relevant tax year.

Paragraph 2. A taxpayer must keep contemporaneous records showing the business purpose, date, and amount of each claimed home office expense.

Paragraph 3. Expenses that are primarily private in nature are not deductible under this article.

#### Article 3.13 — Documentation Duty

Paragraph 1. The taxpayer must retain invoices, usage logs, or equivalent evidence for seven years after filing the return.

Paragraph 2. If documentation is incomplete, the deduction may be denied unless another provision expressly allows estimation.
