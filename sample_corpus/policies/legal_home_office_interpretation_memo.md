# Synthetic Legal Interpretation Memo on Home Office Deduction

## Section 1 — Interpretive Position

Paragraph 1. Legal counsel should interpret home office deductibility by reading current legislation together with the most recent applicable case law on business use and evidentiary sufficiency.

Paragraph 2. Draft legislation and unpublished policy drafts must not be treated as binding authority in legal advice.

## Section 2 — Version Conflicts

Paragraph 1. Where a question targets a year for which no authoritative transition rule is retrieved, legal counsel should abstain or request clarification rather than infer continuity.
