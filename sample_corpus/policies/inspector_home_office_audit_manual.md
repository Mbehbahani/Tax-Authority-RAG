# Synthetic Inspector Home Office Audit Manual

## Section 3 — Audit Questions

Paragraph 1. Inspectors should verify whether the claimed workspace was used predominantly for income-producing activity in the relevant year.

Paragraph 2. Inspectors should request invoices, utility records, floor-plan evidence, and usage logs when the claim depends on proportional allocation.

## Section 5 — Audit Outcome

Paragraph 1. If documentary evidence is incomplete, inspectors may deny the deduction unless another applicable rule explicitly permits estimation.
