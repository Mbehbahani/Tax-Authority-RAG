# Synthetic Helpdesk Home Office Guideline

## Section 2 — Standard Helpdesk Response

Paragraph 3. Helpdesk staff may tell taxpayers that home office expense claims generally require a business purpose and supporting records.

Paragraph 4. Helpdesk staff must not give case-specific legal conclusions and should refer complex disputes to a tax inspector or legal counsel.

## Section 4 — Escalation

Paragraph 2. If the question involves fraud, criminal investigation, or FIOD material, helpdesk staff must not access restricted documents and must escalate through the approved workflow.
